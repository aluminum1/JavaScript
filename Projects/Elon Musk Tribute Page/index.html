Here's an overview of Elon Musk's life and achievements:

  • 1971 - Born in South Africa.
  • 1981 - At age 10, he developed an interest in computing and begins to learn how to program.
  • 1983 - Writes a game in the BASIC language and sell it for $500.
  • 1988 - Graduates from Pretoria Boys High School.
  • 1989 - Just before tuning 18 moves to Canada.
  • 1990 - Attends Queens University in Ontario Canada.
  • 1992 - Transferred to the University of Pennsylvania.
  • 1994 - Received a Bachelor of Science degree in physics at Penn's College of Arts and Sciences, and a Bachelor of Science degree in economics at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
  • 1995 - He Moved to California to begin working towards a PHD in in applied physics at Stanford University, however dropped out to focus on entrepreneurial aspirations.
  • 1995 - Musk co-founded Zip2 web publishing with his brother.
  • 1999 - Compaq Bought Zip2 for $307 million in cash and $34 million in stock options.
  • 1999 - Musk co-founded X.com.
  • 2000 - X.com merged with confinity.
  • 2001 - The new company formed from the merger is called PayPal.com.
  • 2001 - Musk Dreams of sending a greenhouse to mars and begins researching what would become SpaceX.
  • 2002 - Musk becomes a citizen of the United States.
  • 2002 - SpaceX is founded.
  • 2004 - Musk invests in Tesla Motors for the first time.
  • 2006 - Musk Co-founded SolarCity with his cousins Lyndon and Peter Rive.
  • 2008 - Musk is named CEO of Tesla Motors and chief and product architect.
  • 2012 - SolarCity and Tesla Motors begin collaborating and combining their technological advancements.
  • 2013 - Musk release concept plans for a high-speed transportation system called the Hyper-Loop.
  • 2014 - Musk announced that Tesla Motors will allow its technology patents to be used by anyone in good faith in a bid to entice automobile manufacturers to speed up development of electric cars.
  • 2015 - 2015 - Musk announces a Hyper-Loop design competition to take place in August 2016.
  • 2015 - Musk founds OpenAI an artificial intelligence research company.
